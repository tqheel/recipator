PASTA CARBONARA

Autentica Ricetta Italiana

‚Üê Torna ai Percorsi

Informazioni

Prep Time
10 min
Cook Time
15 min
Difficulty
Medium
Servings
4

Ingredienti

400g spaghetti or rigatoni
200g guanciale (or pancetta)
4 large egg yolks
1 whole egg
100g Pecorino Romano, grated
Black pepper, freshly ground
Salt for pasta water

Preparazione

Prepare the Guanciale

Cut the guanciale into small strips or cubes (about 1cm). The fat will render during cooking, so keep the pieces uniform for even cooking.

Boil the Pasta

Bring a large pot of salted water to boil. Add pasta and cook until al dente (usually 1-2 minutes less than package directions). Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining!

Cook the Guanciale

While pasta cooks, add guanciale to a cold pan and heat over medium. Cook until crispy and golden (about 8-10 minutes). No oil needed - the fat will render out. Remove from heat.

Mix Egg and Cheese

In a bowl, whisk together egg yolks, whole egg, and most of the grated Pecorino Romano. Add plenty of freshly ground black pepper. Mix until smooth and creamy.

Combine Everything

Remove the guanciale pan from heat (important!). Add drained pasta to the pan with guanciale. Toss quickly to coat in the rendered fat.

Add the Egg Mixture

Off the heat, quickly pour the egg mixture over the pasta. Toss vigorously! Add reserved pasta water a little at a time to create a creamy, silky sauce. The eggs should NOT scramble!

Serve Immediately

Plate the carbonara immediately. Top with remaining Pecorino Romano and a generous crack of black pepper. Serve hot and enjoy the creamy perfection!

  • NEVER add cream - authentic carbonara is just eggs, cheese, guanciale, and pepper
  • The key is temperature: add eggs to hot pasta OFF the heat to avoid scrambling
  • Guanciale is traditional, but pancetta works as a substitute (never bacon!)
  • Pecorino Romano is essential - Parmigiano can be mixed in but not replace it
  • The pasta water is crucial for creating the creamy sauce consistency
  • Serve immediately - carbonara waits for no one!
